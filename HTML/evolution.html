Tree of evolution

To understand how we humans came to be, we need to understand what came before us. It is for this purpose we have a well-known model known as the Tree of evolution.

Using this model, we can know what originates a certain creature, such as dinosaurs living today as the birds we all see every day. The tree of evolution represents many different creatures. Curious? Go ahead and learn all you want!
