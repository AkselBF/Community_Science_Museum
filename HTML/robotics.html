Robotics

Engineering or robotics are considered one of the most interesting topics among those even remotely interested in the field.

Artificial Intelligence

Controlling technology remotely and getting it to work certainly is something. But how about letting it controll itself?

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.
