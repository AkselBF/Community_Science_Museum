Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person, 40 NOK per children and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.
